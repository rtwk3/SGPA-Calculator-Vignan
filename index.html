SGPA Formula

\[ SGPA = \frac{\sum_{i=1}^{n} (C_i \times P_i)}{\sum_{i=1}^{n} C_i} \]

Where \( C_i \) = Credits of the \( i^{th} \) course,
\( P_i \) = Grade Points of the \( i^{th} \) course
